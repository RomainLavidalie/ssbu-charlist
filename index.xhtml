Hello and welcome onto our Uni Project, called Super Smash Bros. Ultimate Character List,
brought to you by Esteve Déven & Lavidalie Romain from Group 3 !

The goal of this website is to showcase the different playable characters of the Super Smash Bros. Ultimate videogame (made with love by Nintendo).
Like real people, they come from a specific place (or game in this case), and moved to somewhere else when their creators decided to let them fly on their own.
Each character has a set of special moves displayed on their "profile" page.
